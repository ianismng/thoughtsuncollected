Words

drive

"Friends give each other goodbye hugs, riiight?" playfully she asked,
acting dumb.

I smiled an understanding but equally playful smile,
looked over at her as I
turned into the street of her house.

"Naw, you know it's better for both of us if we don't." I said,
hiding how I really felt. The words almost choked me
on the way out.

"Okay..." She replied sadly.

I would've hugged her. I would've hugged her and
never let go.

But I guess the timing just wasn't right.

sweet dreams

you looked at me, smiled, and
said hi back.

thinking about you

just like, everyday

getting over you

One day, i will be able to drive past
these beautiful skylines with you.
It's hard to do anything
that doesn't remind me of you.
Even after all this time, I still
look for you.
And one day, maybe one day,
I can love you like mine again.
