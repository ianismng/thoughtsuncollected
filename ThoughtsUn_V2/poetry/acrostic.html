Poetry

An old dream.

I fill my head with many thoughts,
reminiscing the times we once had.
I look up and see nothing but blue,
surrealism follows my thoughts of you.

I'm showered with memories,
reviving the moments we once shared.
I think of the times passed by,
sinking into what I could never have.

I concede that I'm dumb,
realising I'm not the one.
I don't let that stop me,
slipping further away into the Seine.

Isn't it funny,
realising it's sunny.
Igniting that flame,
slipping into pain.

I don't think it's a coincidence,
reaching out for what we could be.
Improvising the moment,
secretly enjoying every word you speak.

I can't believe I'm writing poetry,
rescuing my thoughts from spiralling.
I see the light shining,
screaming internally but coping.
